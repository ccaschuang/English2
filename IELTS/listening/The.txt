The

The的用法：
1: 溝通雙方都確切的知道所指稱的標的為何，且該標的在世界上不是唯一，因此也可說這裡的the有“限定”的作用。

ex: Open the door

2: The+ 序數/ 最高級/ Only/ same  +名詞

ex: He is the tallest boy in the class.

3: The + 前面出現過的名詞
	ex: My uncle has a son and a daughter. The son is a teacher and the daughter is a doctor.

4: 稱一整個系統或者服務。
	I heard it on the radio.
	You should tell the police

5: The + 形容詞 = 複數普通名詞(有同一屬性的一群人)
	The rich (有錢人)

6: 方向；方位
	the north, the south, the left, the right

7:  集合

國家名稱裡有kingdom, states或republic ex:the United Kingdom; the People’s Republic of China
地理名稱ex:the Himalayas; the Atlantic Ocean; the Amazon
書報、雜誌ex:The Times; The Bible; The Washington Post
知名建築或藝術品ex:the Empire State Building;the Mona Lisa
姓氏複數ex:the Obamas; the Jacksons
全體國民 ex: The Chinese; The Americans

特殊名詞很常見到例外：Exception
1: 國家、都市通常不加The
	ex: Canada, Calgary, Mexico , Philip, China, Taiwan...etc

2: 當國家、地名是屬於一種集合(Collections)的時候, 需要加the (通常後面或是某個集合會有s)
	ex:
		The United States of America (The USA , 但America不需要加)
		The Netherlands 荷蘭 (land的集合)
		The United Arab Emirates
		The United Kingdom (The UK, 英國 = Britian, England (此時不加s))

		the Virigin Islands
		The Philippines 菲律賓群島、菲律賓
3: Countries that have the type of political union in the title use the definite article. 
	The Dominion of Canada,
	The Peoples Republic of China
	The Dominican Republic

其他例外： 有些特殊名詞，已經有約定俗成的例外

ex: The Calgary Tower, 
	Radium Hot Springs

Meals: 通常不加the
	ex: breakfast
		Let's go and have lunch

	但偶而可以加，比方說前一頓 (speicific)
	ex:
		We don't like the dinner.

Jobs: 不加The, 通常只會使用a, an
	ex:  I want to be a software engineer
		 He is a politician.

Proper Nouns:
	ex: June, August, Friday

	See you on Monday
	See you tomorrow
	He is in London

Languages: 永遠不加
	
	He speaks English
	French, Russian 

The only one: 需要加
	ex: The sun, the moon, the earth
 
	He is always on the internet. (網路：永遠要加)
	The moon is beautiful

Uncountable Nouns:  (專屬時加，平常不加)
	I like bread.  (General)
	I like the bread. (specific)
Abstract Nouns (專屬時加，平常不加)
	The information was helpful (specific)
	Freedom is worth dying for (general)


特殊字詞： 不特定指哪一個的時候要加，但是專屬名詞(大寫)時通常不加
airport,
palace
newspaper,
Bank
	ex: Calgary Airport
	I'll go to the airport tonight(加)
	He'll go to Calgary International Airport tonight (不加)

palace
newspaper,
Bank 

但地方名稱的後部包含一個普通名詞（common noun）便用the
	Ex: 
		The Rocky Mountains
		The Niagara Falls（尼加拉瀑布，falls是普通名詞)
		The Pearl River Delta（珠江三角洲，delta是普通名詞）。

其他常看到的：

go to 後面通常不加the
	ex: go to bed, go to church

但可以加the,表示會特別指一個雙方都知道的地點
	go the the church
