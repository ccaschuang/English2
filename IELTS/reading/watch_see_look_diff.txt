	Look & See & watch
look 後面通常直接加! or at
	指眼睛望過去，並且pay attention
	ex look at that dog
See 
	see 表示：notice something using your eye
	三態：see saw seen
	ex: I saw your mom at coffee shop (我看到了妳媽媽(路上看到))

	special : concert, movie, sports game...也都要用see 
		* 這邊指的是你going out to watch something 使用see
		ex: I watched a football match last night.

Watch
	跟Look at很像
	指你look at something for "a longer time"
	且object is moving or changing
	ex: watch TV
		Stay at the coffee shop and do people watching

ex: 
	Last night I saw The Shawshank Redemption (刺激1995)
		我"去電影院"看刺激1995
	Last night I watched The Shawshank Redemption (刺激1995) 
		我"在家"看刺激1995

ex2: 
	I watched a man digging a hole
		指我坐在那邊，看那個男人挖洞(時間持續下去)
	I saw a man digging a hole
		我看到有個男人在那邊挖洞 (指比方說走路經過的時候看到的。)
