1: 不管哪類型的信件：都可以分為normal/semi-formal/informal三大類

              formal            semi-formal          informal

opening：    Dear Sir/Madam		Dear Mr. Brown		 Dear John
                               	Dear Ms. Stone       Dear Susan

opening的稱謂：會對應到如何close, 其中formal的信件最常是寫給公司(你不知道對方名稱)


close		Yours				Yours				Best Regards,
			faithfully			sincerely			Warn wishes

(結尾使用)

start the letter apporpriately

formal/semi-informal
	I am writing to inquire about...
	I am writing to inform you...
	I am writing in connection with...

Informal:
	I hope you and your family are all well.
	How have you been? It's been too long since we were last in touch

Standard expression:
	(可以注意一下formal/informal的語氣不同)
	My sincere apologies for ... (missing a meeting) /I am very sorry for (missing your wedding)
	I'd be greatful if you could.../Could you please


Key tips
	Understand the scoring criteria
		．Task achievement --do all, give full response, 150 words
		．coherence + cohension
			--present ideas logically
			--use paragraphs
			--use standard expressions
		．Lexical resources
			--use vocabulary naturally
			--use correct spelling
		．Grammer range + accuracy (文章需要使用複雜句，與部份較困難的單字)
			--varied grammer
			--difficult sentenses
			--punctuation + capitalization (大小寫要特別注意)
